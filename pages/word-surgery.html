Word Surgery

Identifying Word Parts in Medical Terms

The purpose of this activity is to practice and apply your ability to comprehend how language building occurs in Medical Terminology by viewing a full medical term and breaking it down into it's  individual word parts.  By doing so you will be able to demonstrate your understanding of how the individual parts make up the larger term and how this affects spelling, definition, and overall communication when completed accurately and correctly. 

Task

  1. Download the Word Surgery Worksheet.
  2. For each of the terms listed, apply the rules of medical language to analyze, define, and build the words in the word list. 
  3. Save your work. 
  4. Submit the completed worksheet to the appropriate Dropbox. 

Expectations

  • Each of the tables should be correctly completed. 
  • For each term you should follow the steps to analyze, define, and build each term.
