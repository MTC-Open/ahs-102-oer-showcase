Vocab Lab

Unit 2

The purpose of this activity is to build upon your recognition and mastery of the comprehension of word parts, their definitions, as well as their abbreviations. The ability to memorize the definitions or abbreviations of medical terms are important tasks when learning the language of Medical Terminology. This assignment will challenge you to place those full medical terms and their abbreviations into full sentences. This will allow you to demonstrate your true comprehension of these medical terms as they relate to the human body, as well as put them into a greater context of how they apply to everyday life situations. 

Task

This assignment has two (2) parts. Please submit as a single document or submission. 

Part 1: Definitions of Terms Using Word Parts

  1. Select five (5) Medical Terms from the unit terms below.
  2. On a Word document, type the term.
  3. Type the term broken into its word parts (marked with slashes)
  4. Use the word appropriately in a sentence (the sentence cannot be the definition of the word and should show your understanding of the meaning). 

Example: 

Term: Hepatitis
Broken into word parts: hepat/o + -itis
Sentence with term: Sharing dirty needles can lead to a form of hepatitis. 

Part 2: Abbreviations

  1. Select five (5) abbreviations from the unit listed below.
  2. Type the abbreviation from the chapter.
  3. Type the term of the abbreviation.
  4. Create a sentence using the abbreviation term correctly in the sentence (>3 words in the sentence).  Put some thought into the sentence so that they help define the term.  DO NOT use the definition of the term as your sentence.

Example:  

RA – Rheumatoid Arthritis - Gail has RA and her doctor told her that her muscle atrophy and muscle weakness is due to her inactivity during the day.

Term List (Select 5 terms):

    • Arthroplasty
    • Biopsy
    • Costochondritis
    • Dermatologist
    • Melanocyte
    • Onynchophagia
    • Osteoarthritis
    • Osteopenia
    • Rhytidectomy
    • Scoliosis

Abbreviations List (Select 5 abbreviations): 

    • BCC
    • bx
    • derm
    • fx
    • MRI
    • OA
    • PT
    • RA
    • RBC
    • WBC

Save & Submit
