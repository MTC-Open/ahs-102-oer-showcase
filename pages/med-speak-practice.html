Med Speak Practice

This selected excerpt shows a pronunciation practice activity designed to help students use medical terminology clearly.

Med Speak

Practice

Throughout your time in this course, you will be tasked with completing pronunciation assignments. The purpose of this activity is to test your ability to complete pronunciation assignments successfully. 

Task

Note: You will submit this assignment as an audio recording per the Pronunciation Assignment Instructions. 

  1. At the beginning of the recording, say your first and last name. Then announce this is for the practice submission. 
    • For example: "India Jones, Practice".
  2. Speak the terms in the order listed. Say the number then correctly pronounce the term clearly using conversational speech.
  3. Submit your work to the Practice Dropbox folder. 
    • Note: You can complete this practice as many times as you like. It is recommended you complete the practice with each computer/device you will be using to complete future coursework. Your submission will count as a single grade item. 

List of terms to practice

1.    intravenous 
2.    osteoarthritis  
3.    osteoarthropathy 

Expectations

  • Speak clearly using conversational speech. 
  • Your audio recording should be distinct, clear, and of acceptable quality. Preview your recording before submitting.
  • Don't forget to announce your name and "practice" at the beginning of the recording.
