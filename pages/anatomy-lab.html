Anatomy Lab

Now that you have learned the specific parts of the body being referenced and how those specific parts relate to the entire body as a whole, you can apply this knowledge. The purpose of this activity is to identify the systems of the body and their major functions as well as identify the anatomic position, body planes, regions, and cavities. 

Identification Quiz

For this lab activity, you will complete an identification quiz where you will be asked to identify organ systems of the human body as well as use the anatomic reference system to identify the following: 

  • Body planes.
  • Body cavities.
  • Directional terms.

After reading the description below, follow the link to complete the quiz. 

Description

The Identification Quiz consists of five (5) labeling activities/questions. 

Point values of questions is based on the number of items you need to identify as well as the overall difficulty. 

The quiz is untimed so you can take as much time as needed to complete the activity. 

You have a single attempt.
