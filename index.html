AHS 102 OER Course Redesign Showcase

This public showcase highlights selected student-facing examples from an exemplary AHS 102 Medical Terminology course redesign. The course uses Open Educational Resources to support affordability, access, clear navigation, active practice, and student-centered learning.

About This Showcase

This site includes selected public-facing excerpts from the course design. It does not include student records, gradebook content, restricted LMS materials, assessment answer keys, or private course interactions.

How to Read the Student Success Data

Outcome comparisons reflect available student success data through Spring 2025 and use a fall-to-fall comparison window for AHS 102. Results should be interpreted cautiously because student outcomes may also be influenced by enrollment patterns, course modality, instructor mix, student preparation, and broader course redesign efforts.
